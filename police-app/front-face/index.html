Opening up university police logs

This dashboard makes it easy to look up crime statistics reported by universities in the Washington, D.C. and Maryland area, without having to parse through dense PDFs, Google Drive folders and websites.

Updated automatically at 9 a.m. every Monday. Read more about how we developed the dashboard.
